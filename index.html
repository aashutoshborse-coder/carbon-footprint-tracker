Green Tips

Eco-Friendly Living Tips

Simple everyday actions that collectively make a massive positive impact on our planet.

Save Electricity

Switch off lights and appliances when not in use. Use LED bulbs and smart power strips. Unplug chargers when not charging.

Saves ~200kg CO₂/yr

Reduce Food Waste

Plan meals, store food properly, compost scraps, and buy only what you need. Food waste accounts for 8% of global emissions.

Saves ~150kg CO₂/yr

Use Public Transport

Taking the bus or train instead of driving can reduce your transport emissions by up to 75% per journey.

Saves ~1.5T CO₂/yr

Plant Trees

A single tree absorbs ~21 kg of CO₂ per year. Support reforestation projects or plant native trees in your community.

Absorbs ~21kg CO₂/tree/yr

Recycle

Properly sort and recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metal. Recycling aluminum uses 95% less energy than making it fresh.

Saves ~100kg CO₂/yr

Save Water

Take shorter showers, fix leaks promptly, and install water-efficient fixtures. Water treatment is energy-intensive.

Saves ~50kg CO₂/yr

Renewable Energy

Switch to a green energy tariff or install solar panels. Renewable energy eliminates household electricity emissions.

Saves ~1.4T CO₂/yr

Cloth Bags

Replace single-use plastic bags with reusable cloth bags. One reusable bag replaces ~700 plastic bags over its lifetime.

Saves ~30kg CO₂/yr
Level Up

More Green Actions

Go beyond the basics — these powerful lifestyle shifts create the biggest environmental impact.

High Impact
Saves ~0.5T CO₂/yr

Go Plant-Based

Switching to a plant-based diet even 3 days a week significantly cuts the greenhouse gases linked to meat and dairy production. It's one of the most impactful personal choices you can make.

  • Reduces land and water use
  • Lowers methane emissions
  • Supports biodiversity
Fashion
Saves ~300kg CO₂/yr

Shop Second-Hand

Fast fashion is one of the world's biggest polluters. Buying second-hand, renting, or swapping clothes drastically reduces textile waste and factory emissions.

  • Reduces textile factory waste
  • Saves water (1 jeans = 7,500L)
  • Circular economy model
Community
Multiplied impact

Community Action

Join or organise local clean-up drives, community gardens, and awareness campaigns. Collective action amplifies individual effort and drives policy-level change.

  • Local clean-up events
  • Community solar programmes
  • Influence local policy
Digital
Saves ~65kg CO₂/yr

Go Paperless

Switch to e-bills, digital documents, and e-books. Unsubscribe from junk mail and opt for digital receipts to reduce paper demand and deforestation.

  • E-statements and e-invoices
  • Digital note-taking
  • Reduces deforestation pressure
